About Nexoin Stone Rings

Established in 2010, Nexoin International specializes in providing unique silver gemstone rings with a wide variety for men. Our collections include Turkish historical pieces, Arabic unique designs, Italian latest styles, Hindi traditional rings, and local handmade creations.

We offer custom crafting services - customers can request any stone or silver ring design to be made especially for them. Our unique 65% money-back guarantee allows customers to get $65 back from every $100 spent at any time, whether for the ring or stone.
